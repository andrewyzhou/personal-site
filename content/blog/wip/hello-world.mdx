i deleted instagram. i liked the photos but not the feed, so i'm moving that part here instead — a small corner of my own site where i can post pictures, write a little, and not be optimized at.

expect a mix: trips and climbs and runs with too many photos, notes on things i'm building, and the occasional half-formed thought i wanted to get out of my head.

## how this works

posts are plain markdown. images get captions and lazy-loaded, optimized rendering automatically — see `content/blog/README.md` for how to add them. drafts live in `wip/` until they're ready.
