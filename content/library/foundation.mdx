published in 1951 as a fix-up of stories originally serialized in *astounding science fiction*, *foundation* is the opening novel of isaac asimov's foundation series. it introduces psychohistory — a fictional mathematics of mass human behavior — and hari seldon's plan to shorten the coming galactic dark age from thirty millennia to one through the founding of an encyclopedia-keeping colony at the empire's periphery.
