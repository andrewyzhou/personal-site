*huberman lab essentials* is a spin-off feed from stanford neurobiologist andrew huberman's main podcast, condensing full-length episodes into ~30-minute distillations of the most actionable, peer-reviewed protocols on each topic — sleep, light exposure, dopamine, focus, exercise, recovery, and so on.
