the meditation section of dr. k's guide to mental health. dr. k (alok kanojia) trained as a hindu monk before becoming a psychiatrist, and this module pulls from that lineage to teach meditation as a structured practice with a clear progression — concentration, awareness, equanimity — rather than the watered-down "calm app" version most people encounter.
