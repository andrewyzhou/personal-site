the adhd section of dr. k's guide to mental health — a long-form video course from alok kanojia (psychiatrist, founder of healthygamergg) that combines clinical psychiatry, neuroscience, and concepts from yogic / vedic traditions. the adhd module covers how dopamine dynamics shape attention, why modern environments amplify symptoms, and what self-directed practices (meditation, environment design, motivation systems) can do to help.
