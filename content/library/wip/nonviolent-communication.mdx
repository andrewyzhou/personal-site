first published in 1999 and developed by psychologist marshall rosenberg over decades of mediation work, *nonviolent communication* (nvc) lays out a way of speaking and listening that separates observations from judgments, surfaces underlying feelings and needs, and turns demands into requests. used widely in conflict resolution, parenting, and therapy.
