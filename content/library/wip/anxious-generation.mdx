---

published in 2024, *the anxious generation* makes the case that the simultaneous rise of smartphone-based childhood and decline of independent free play has triggered an unprecedented mental health crisis in adolescents born after ~1996. haidt — a social psychologist at nyu stern — combines large-scale longitudinal data with developmental psychology to propose four norms (no smartphones before high school, no social media before 16, phone-free schools, more independence in the real world).
